Tech Mystery

The event would have participants explore a mysterious room where the clues to the culprit of a devious deeds are hidden. Participants would have to use their wits, judgement and above all else, technical skills to decipher the clues & puzzles left, racing against the clock to catch the culprit. Multiple ends leading to different scenarios, will our participants be able to solve the mystery & save the day?

Rules and Regulations:

  • Conducted by offline mode.
  • Participation:Team (2 – 4 members).
  • Registration fee: 100/member

Organized by:

Coding Blocks Club

Bhopal University
